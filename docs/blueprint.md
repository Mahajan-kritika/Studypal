# **App Name**: Study Journey

## Core Features:

- Personalized Study Plan: AI-powered tool creates customized study schedules based on the user's goals, deadlines, and learning pace.
- Intelligent Explanations: The system breaks down complex topics into easy-to-understand concepts, offering various learning formats (text, video, diagrams).
- Practice Test Generator: Automatically generates practice quizzes and exams tailored to the user's study plan and learning history.
- Progress Tracker: Monitors the user's performance and provides visual reports of their progress, highlighting strengths and weaknesses.
- Login System: Secure user authentication and profile management.
- Analytics Dashboard: Provides a visual representation of study habits and learning outcomes. It will also show a visual guide about daily and weakly goals for success in the field
- Weekly Progress Report: Generates downloadable reports summarizing weekly learning progress, with the insights from the AI models including area of improvement for user.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Indigo (#4B0082) to evoke a sense of wisdom, focus, and sophistication, ideal for creating a productive learning environment.
- Secondary color: Warm Beige (#F5F5DC) to provide a neutral and calming backdrop, reducing eye strain and promoting extended periods of concentration.
- Accent color: Muted Gold (#CD7F32) for highlighting key interactive elements and progress indicators, adding a touch of elegance and motivation.
- Body and headline font: 'Montserrat', a versatile sans-serif font that offers a clean, modern aesthetic and excellent readability.
